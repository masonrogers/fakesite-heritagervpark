Welcome to Heritage RV Park

Heritage RV Park is located in beautiful Augusta, GA. With 3 minute access to I-20 and close proximity to dozens of restaurants, gas stations, and department stores, your stay here at Heritage will be accommodating and pleasant. We are a big-rig friendly facility and provide you with plenty of room to maneuver easily around our park.


All of our sites feature:

Aluminum/wood picnic tables

Full hookup including 50-amp service

High-Speed and Reliable WiFi


Our new premium sites add the following:

Aluminum picnic table

Adjacent beautiful landscaping
